Antibiotics are the most common treatment for bronchiectasis.
Oral antibiotics are suggested for most cases, but harder to treat infections may require intravenous (IV) antibiotics. 
Macrolides are a specific type of antibiotics that not only kill certain types of bacteria but also reduce inflammation in the bronchi.


Prevention:

have had the vaccines for measles and whooping cough. 
Get annual flu vaccinations and advised pneumonia vaccinations. 
Stay away from harmful substances that can damage your lungs, such as smoke, gases, and fumes
