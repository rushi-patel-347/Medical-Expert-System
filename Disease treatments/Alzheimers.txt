Aducanumab is the only disease-modifying medication currently approved to treat Alzheimer's.
This medication is a human antibody, or immunotherapy, that targets the protein beta-amyloid and helps to reduce amyloid plaques, which are brain lesions associated with Alzheimer's.

Prevention:
stopping smoking.

keeping alcohol to a minimum.

eating a healthy, 

balanced diet, (at least 5 portions of fruit and vegetables every day)

exercising for at least 150 minutes every week 
moderate-intensity aerobic activity (such as cycling or fast walking)
