Prevention:

Eat a gluten-free diet to prevent symptoms and damage to the small intestine. 
Even a small amount of gluten may cause damage

Avoid all foods that contain wheat, rye, and barley.

Foods that are often made with these grains include bread, bagels, pasta, pizza, malted breakfast cereals, and crackers

Mediacation:

There is no medication that treats celiac disease. To avoid the health problems that it can cause, you'll need to go completely gluten-free
